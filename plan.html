The Rules
01

Test Before Every Send

Every email batch gets a test to Meirharush10@gmail.com first. You confirm it's perfect. Only then it goes out. No exceptions — ever.

02

One Call Per Day Per Contact

Morning only (9–11am). Log the result before moving to the next call. No answer = WhatsApp message, not a second call same day.

03

Clean Lists Only

Before any outreach: verify emails, remove bounced addresses, deduplicate. No watch stores. No corporate chains. No info@ addresses.

04

No PDF on First Email

First email goal = get a phone call. Send the catalogue link only after they express interest. Attachments in cold email go straight to spam.

05

Log Everything, Same Day

Every call, email, and meeting goes in the CRM the day it happens. No memory = no pipeline = no closed deals. The system only works if you feed it.

06

You Approve Before Anything Goes Out

Nothing gets sent, posted, or shared without your explicit go-ahead. Every client touchpoint is under your control. No surprises.
