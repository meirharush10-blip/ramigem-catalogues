Operating Rules
Non-Negotiable Rules
01

Verify Before You Send

Every email gets a test to Meirharush10@gmail.com first. Confirmed perfect → send. No exceptions. Ever.

02

One Call Per Day Per Contact

Morning only (9–11am). Log the result. If no answer: WhatsApp text. Never call the same number twice in one day.

03

Clean Lead Lists

Before any outreach: remove bounced addresses, verify emails, deduplicate by phone. No watch/clock stores. No corporate chains.

04

No PDF on First Email

First email goal = phone call. Send the catalogue link only after they express interest. PDFs in cold email go to spam.

05

Log Everything in CRM

Every call, every email, every meeting goes in the CRM the same day. No memory = no pipeline = no deals.

06

Meir Approves Before Anything Goes Out

Nothing gets sent, posted, or shared without your explicit "go ahead." You are in full control of every client touchpoint.
